ABOUT PSALMTOX

Psalmtox is a God-given vision conceived as a teenager. She first began in 2007 as a group of boys called The Happy Brothers, later regrouped as God's generals in 2010 and up till now the visionary didn't stop the delivery process. The visionary is known for his inspirational and energetic performances on stage taking worshippers on a journey. He has worked with notable gospel Music Artists, Producer. He his a song writer, composer, music administrator, consultant and a teacher. The Ministry currently run a Music Institute with graduating students since 2020. He is currently a Worship leader at the Communion Christian Center by his spiritual father (Pst. Tope Awofisayo) He is currently the Worship coordinator in the church. He attributes his early christian lifestyle to his mentor who trained him earlier in ministry Pst. Tope Adeyi.
